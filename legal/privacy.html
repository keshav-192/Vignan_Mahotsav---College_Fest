VIGNAN MAHOTSAV

Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Vignan Mahotsav collects, uses, and protects the information you provide while using this website and participating in the event.

1. Information We Collect

  • Registration details such as name, MHID, email, phone number, college, and date of birth.
  • Event registration information including selected events and participation details.
  • Feedback or messages you submit through the feedback form.
  • Basic technical information such as IP address and browser details (used for security and analytics).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Mahotsav account and MHID.
  • To register you for events and manage event-related communication.
  • To improve the event experience based on feedback and usage.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the website and event systems.

3. Data Sharing

Your information is used only by the Mahotsav organizing team for event-related purposes. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Limited data may be shared with internal departments or authorized partners strictly for organizing and managing the event.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is fully secure, and you should keep your MHID and password confidential.

5. Your Choices

You can update certain information from your profile page after logging in. If you have any concerns about your data or want specific changes, please contact the organizing team.

6. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Mahotsav organizers via the contact details given on the official website.
